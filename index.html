Gabriela Karoline Michelon

I am currently working in Software Product Line Engineering with 5 years of experience and serve as a Project Manager for AI-driven Product Development at Marquardt in Frankfurt am Main, Germany.
I earned my PhD in Computer Science in 2022 from Johannes Kepler University Linz, Austria, where I also worked as a researcher at the Institute for Software Systems Engineering and LIT Secure and Correct Systems Lab.
I received my Master's degree in Computational Technologies for Agribusiness in 2018 from the Federal Technological University of Paraná - Campus Medianeira, Brazil, including a three-month period at the University of California, Davis, USA.
I completed a postgraduate course in Software Engineering in 2018 at Univel University Center, Cascavel, Brazil.
I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in 2016 from the Federal Technological University of Paraná – Campus Medianeira, Brazil.
