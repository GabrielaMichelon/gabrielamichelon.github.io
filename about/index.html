About

Doctor in Computer Science at Johannes Kepler University Linz in November 03, 2022. Master in Computational Technologies for Agribusiness in 2018 at the Federal Technological University of Paraná - Campus Medianeira with sandwich period of three months in University of California, Davis. Work as Substitute Professor from November 2018 to April 2019 at the Computer Science Department at the Federal Technological University of Paraná, Campus Medianeira. Currently is a Researcher at the Institute for Software Systems Engineering and LIT Secure and Correct Systems Lab. Graduated in Computer Science in 2016 at the University Federal Technological of Paraná – Campus Medianeira, and concluded graduation studies in Software Engineering in 2018. Research Areas of Interest: Artificial Intelligence, Software Engineering, Software Product Line, Software Reverse Engineering, Geographic Database, and Computational applications to Agribusiness.
